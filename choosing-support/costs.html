What Does Tutoring Typically Cost?

This article provides information on the typical costs associated with different types of learning support.

Individual Tutoring

One-on-one sessions range from $40 to $80 per hour, depending on the subject and tutor's experience. Packages may reduce the rate.

Group Tutoring

Group sessions are often $20 to $50 per hour per student, making them more affordable for families.

Online Options

Online tutoring can be similar in cost but may include platform fees. Some services offer subscriptions for unlimited access.

Additional Costs

Consider materials, travel, or assessments. Free or subsidized options may be available through community programs.
