Provider Mini-Profiles

Kip McGrath Education

Overview: Established international brand with centres across Australia. All tutors are qualified teachers (no student tutors), providing professional expertise in every session. Offers English and maths support for primary and secondary students, with small group sessions in-centre or online. Focus on diagnostic assessment, individual learning plans, and regular progress tracking. Many families value the consistency and experience of teacher-led support.

Best for: Parents seeking structured, teacher-led support and clear communication about progress.

NumberWorks'nWords

Overview: Specialises in maths and English tuition for Years 1–10. Uses a mix of qualified teachers and trained tutors. Small group sessions in learning centres, with personalised programs and regular feedback to parents.

Best for: Students needing targeted help in maths or English in a supportive, centre-based environment.

Kumon

Overview: International franchise focused on self-paced maths and English learning. Not all instructors are qualified teachers. Students complete daily worksheets at home and attend brief centre visits for guidance and assessment.

Best for: Families wanting independent, routine-based learning and long-term skill development.

Fruition Tuition

Overview: Australian-owned company offering a wide range of subjects with both qualified teachers and trained tutors. Provides individual and small group sessions, both in-centre and online. Emphasises confidence-building and tailored support.

Best for: Students who benefit from flexible formats and a personalised approach.

Cluey

Overview: Online-only platform with qualified teachers and uni students. Offers 1-to-1 and small group sessions in various subject. Uses adaptive technology and provides detailed progress reports.

Best for: Families seeking convenient, online tutoring with reporting and flexibility.
