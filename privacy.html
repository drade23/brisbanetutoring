Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information on the North Brisbane & Moreton Bay Education Guide website.

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from visitors. You can browse all content without providing any personal details.

2. No Cookies or Tracking

This site does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts. Your browsing activity is not monitored or recorded by us.

3. Search Functionality

All search features are handled locally in your browser. No search queries or results are sent to any server or third party.

4. External Links

Our site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. Please review their privacy policies if you visit them.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@northbrisbaneeducationguide.com.au.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
